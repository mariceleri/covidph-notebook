Philippine COVID-19 Cases

Feb 8, 2020 - Present

This is a choropleth map that shows the relative COVID-19 cases between the municipalities and cities of the Philippines. The cases are normalized with the population of each local government boundary. This is to help the viewer compare cases with connection to population densities.

This map aims to help the viewer find patterns and gain insights on the spread of the disease and the effects of the community and goverment efforts. This only provides a "big picture" and should not be used as basis for any decision.

All the data is free and comes from the public domain. It is entered manually and this map may not be always up to date.

Sources:

Department of Health, Philippines
National Statistics Office, 2015 Census
